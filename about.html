About Us

The Habiganj Association of Toronto was established with a vision to unite and empower individuals originating from Habiganj, Bangladesh, and now residing in Toronto, Canada. The association serves as a bridge connecting members of the community, fostering relationships, and supporting cultural and social activities.

Our mission is to create a vibrant community where members can share their experiences, celebrate their heritage, and support each other in times of need. The Habiganj Association of Toronto organizes events, seminars, and celebrations to preserve and promote Bangladeshi culture while integrating into Canadian society.

We believe in inclusivity, diversity, and collaboration. By bringing people together, we aim to create opportunities for personal and professional growth while maintaining our cultural roots. Together, we strive to build a better future for our community.
